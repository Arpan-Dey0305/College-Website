About Us

Abacus Institute of Engineering and Management provides you an exceptional educational environment of full attention that you deserve. The campus is perfect fit for you where you will get a chance to transform yourself and in turn transform the world as well.

The ABACUS family came into being in 2008 and has since evolved into a leading institute under JIS Group and Techno India Group, offering quality education and industry-ready programs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission criteria?

Admissions are based on merit and entrance exams as per AIEM norms.

Does the college provide hostel facilities?

Yes, separate hostels for boys and girls with modern amenities.

What courses are offered in CSE?

B.Tech, M.Tech, and research programs in Computer Science.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, merit-based and need-based scholarships are offered.

What is the fee structure?

The fee structure varies by program; details available on the official site.

Are internships provided?

Yes, AIEM facilitates internships in reputed companies.

What are the library facilities?

A modern library with digital access and reading rooms.

Is campus Wi-Fi available?

Yes, campus-wide high-speed Wi-Fi is available for students.

Are sports facilities available?

Yes, facilities for indoor and outdoor games are provided.

What is the placement record?

AIEM has an excellent placement record with top companies visiting campus.

Are there cultural activities?

Yes, various fests, workshops, and clubs are active on campus.

What transport facilities are available?

Bus services and connectivity via train and road are available.

Are labs well-equipped?

Yes, AIEM has state-of-the-art labs for practical learning.

Is there an alumni network?

Yes, alumni are connected through events and mentoring programs.

Can students participate in research projects?

Yes, AIEM encourages research and provides guidance and resources.
